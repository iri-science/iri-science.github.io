The Integrated Reseach Infrastructure (IRI) is a new Department of Energy (DOE) Office of Science program encompassing all ASCR Facilities and participating domain partners

The IRI program involves the whole Advanced Scientific Computing Research (ASCR) Facilities ecosystem (and potentially, the broader DOE research computing ecosystem)

DOE Facility Interface Improvement

IRI is being designed to create better interfaces between DOE user facilities

Integrated Frameworks for IRI

IRI will include frameworks for integration in software, policy and governance

Enhancing Facility Capabilities

IRI will layer on top of existing (and future) facility capabilities, to enhance integration and usability/user experience

Managing ASCR Integration

IRI is the means by which we will manage the ASCR ecosystem and its further integration that is required specifically for the Office of Science
