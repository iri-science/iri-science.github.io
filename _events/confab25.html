There will be multiple IRI presentations at ESnet's upcoming Confab25 conference running April 7-9, 2025. Confab is ESnet's largest user meeting, bringing together domain scientists, network engineers, DOE program managers, user facility staff, and ESnet staff to co-design the future of integrated science. Confab25 will include updates from ESnet, our scientific research collaborators, IRI, and more. This is a great opportunity to connect with IRI stakeholders from across the DOE Office of Science ecosystem.
